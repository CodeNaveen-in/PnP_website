How do I join Pixels & Panels? keyboard_arrow_down

Fill the membership form linked in the Join Us page or email us at pixelsnpanels.society@study.iitm.ac.in.

What kind of events do you run? keyboard_arrow_down

We run screenings, panels, workshops and community meetups focused on cinema and animation.

Can I propose a screening? keyboard_arrow_down

Yes — propose via email or message us on our socials. We consider community suggestions each semester.
